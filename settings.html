How We Calculate Water Usage

WaterWise estimates water consumption based on the length of ChatGPT responses and research from 2024-2025 studies.

Research-Based Estimates:

  • GPT-3: ~500ml per 10-50 responses (10-50ml per response)
  • GPT-4: ~519ml per 100-word response
  • GPT-4o: ~2.3-39ml per medium response (150-200 words)
  • 2025 Models: As low as 0.26ml per query

Why Such Variation?

Water consumption varies significantly based on:

  • Data center location: Hot climates need more cooling
  • Cooling technology: Some systems are 10-25x more efficient
  • Model size: Larger models consume more resources
  • Season: Summer requires more cooling than winter
  • Infrastructure: Newer data centers are more water-efficient

Our Approach:

We use a conservative mid-range estimate to balance accuracy with awareness:

  • Default: 15ml per 100 words
  • This accounts for modern efficiency improvements
  • Considers average data center conditions
  • You can customize this below based on your preference
