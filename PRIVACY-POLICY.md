# Privacy Policy for WaterWise

**Last Updated**: January 13, 2026

## Overview

WaterWise ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our Chrome extension that estimates and visualizes water consumption for ChatGPT conversations.

**In short**: WaterWise does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data stays on your computer.

---

## Information Collection and Use

### What Data We Store Locally

WaterWise stores the following data **locally on your device** using Chrome's storage API:

1. **User Preferences**
   - Custom water calculation rate (ml per word setting)
   - Floating button display preference (on/off)

2. **Usage Statistics** (Anonymous and Local Only)
   - Total water consumption (calculated in milliliters/liters)
   - Number of active chat conversations tracked
   - Total message count across conversations
   - Per-conversation metadata:
     - Anonymous chat ID (generated by ChatGPT URL)
     - Water usage total for that conversation
     - Message count for that conversation
     - Last updated timestamp
     - Chat URL (for navigation purposes only)

### What We DO NOT Collect

WaterWise does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit:

- ❌ Message content or conversation text
- ❌ Personal information (name, email, age, etc.)
- ❌ Authentication credentials or passwords
- ❌ Browsing history outside of ChatGPT URLs
- ❌ Location data or IP addresses
- ❌ Financial information
- ❌ Health information
- ❌ Any personally identifiable information (PII)

---

## How Data is Stored

All data is stored using **Chrome's local storage API** on your device. This means:

- ✅ Data never leaves your computer
- ✅ No external servers or databases are used
- ✅ No analytics or tracking services are employed
- ✅ No third-party data sharing occurs
- ✅ You have full control over your data

---

## Data Access and Control

### Viewing Your Data

All stored data can be viewed through:
- The WaterWise dashboard (click the extension icon)
- Chrome DevTools → Application → Storage → Local Storage

### Deleting Your Data

You can delete all WaterWise data at any time by:

1. **Using the Extension**:
   - Click the WaterWise extension icon
   - Click "Clear Data" button at the bottom
   - Confirm the action

2. **Uninstalling the Extension**:
   - Go to `chrome://extensions/`
   - Click "Remove" on WaterWise
   - All local data will be automatically deleted

3. **Manually**:
   - Open Chrome DevTools (F12)
   - Go to Application → Storage → Local Storage
   - Clear WaterWise entries

## How the Extension Works

### Content Script Functionality

WaterWise uses content scripts to:

1. **Read** AI response text on ChatGPT pages (`chatgpt.com` and `chat.openai.com`)
2. **Count** the number of words in each response
3. **Calculate** estimated water usage based on your settings
4. **Display** water usage badges below AI responses
5. **Store** anonymous statistics locally for the dashboard

### Permissions Explanation

| Permission | Why We Need It | What We Do NOT Do |
|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| `storage` | Save your preferences and statistics locally | We do NOT sync data to cloud or external servers |
| `activeTab` | Display water badges on ChatGPT pages | We do NOT track your browsing or collect page content |
| `https://chatgpt.com/*`<br>`https://chat.openai.com/*` | Inject badges on ChatGPT specifically | We do NOT access any other websites or collect conversation content |
